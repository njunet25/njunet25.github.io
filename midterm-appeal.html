Midterm Exam Scores and Appeal Notice

Dear Students,

The midterm exam scores and graded papers have now been released. Please use NJU table to check your score and review your paper details promptly.

Appeal Process and Requirements

We understand that errors may occasionally occur during the grading process. If you have any discrepancy regarding your midterm exam score (e.g., missing points for a typo, or perceived misgrading/misjudgment), please strictly follow the requirements below to submit your appeal within the stated period:

  1. Submission Method: All appeals must be submitted via email to the Teaching Assistants.
  2. Email Subject Line: Please use a clear subject line, such as: [Midterm] - Your Name - NJU ID.
  3. Content Requirements:
    • Clearly describe your reason for the appeal and the specific point(s) of contention (e.g., "For Question X, Part Y, I believe I should have received A points, but was given B points because...").
    • You MUST attach clear photographs or scans of the specific section(s) of your graded paper that are being appealed as supporting evidence.

Review and Score Modification

Upon receiving your email, the TAs will conduct a re-evaluation based on your request and the provided evidence. If the appeal is verified as valid, your final score will be adjusted accordingly.

  • The TAs aim to process all appeals within 1-2 business days of receipt.
  • If you do not receive a reply within 3 business days, please send a gentle reminder (via email or QQ) to the TAs.

Appeal Deadline

The appeal period is two weeks. The absolute deadline for all appeals is: Sunday, December 21, 2025.

Please note: Appeals submitted after the deadline will not be accepted. After this date, we will consider that all students are satisfied with their midterm exam grading.
